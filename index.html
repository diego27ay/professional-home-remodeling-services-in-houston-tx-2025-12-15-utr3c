Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our remodeling services

How long does a typical kitchen remodel take?

Most kitchen remodels take between 4-8 weeks, depending on the scope of work and any unforeseen issues discovered during demolition. Smaller updates might be completed in 2-3 weeks, while comprehensive remodels with structural changes could take 10-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the project.

What is included in your initial consultation?

Our initial consultation is completely free and typically lasts 45-60 minutes. During this time, we'll discuss your project goals, budget, timeline, and design preferences. We'll take measurements, assess the current condition of the space, and identify any potential challenges. We'll also provide preliminary recommendations and answer any questions you have about our process. After the consultation, we'll prepare a detailed proposal with accurate cost estimates.

Do you provide design services, or should I hire a designer separately?

We offer comprehensive design consultation as part of our service. Our experienced team can help you select materials, finishes, layouts, and color schemes that match your vision and budget. We work closely with you to ensure every design decision reflects your personal style. If you already have a designer or specific design plans, we're happy to work with those as well. Our flexibility allows us to accommodate various project approaches.

What is your payment schedule and do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a 50% deposit to secure your project start date and begin material ordering. The remaining balance is typically divided into progress payments as work is completed, with the final payment due upon project completion. This payment structure protects both parties and ensures proper project management. We're happy to discuss flexible payment arrangements for larger projects to meet your financial needs.

Do you offer any warranties on your work?

Absolutely. We provide a comprehensive 2-year warranty on all labor and workmanship. Additionally, all materials and appliances come with manufacturer warranties, which we'll transfer to you. We stand behind our work and are committed to your satisfaction. If any issues arise after project completion, we'll address them promptly at no additional cost to you.
